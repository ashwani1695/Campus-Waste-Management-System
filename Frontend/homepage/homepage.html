About Us

Welcome to the Campus Waste Management System of Tezpur University — a digital initiative aimed at promoting sustainability and responsible waste practices within our campus. This platform is developed by students of the university with a vision to streamline waste collection, increase awareness, and foster eco-friendly habits among the campus community. Our mission is to make Tezpur University a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable campus through smart solutions. From identifying waste hotspots to enabling real-time reporting and promoting recycling practices, our system is built to empower students, staff, and authorities to take collective action. We believe that small changes can lead to big impacts. Together, let’s build a waste-free campus for a better tomorrow.
